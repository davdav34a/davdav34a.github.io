Alcoholic Drinks

Wines

  • Merlot 175 ml:
    A smooth and fruity red wine known for its velvety texture and flavors of black cherry, plum, and chocolate, making it a popular choice among wine lovers.
  • Cabernet Sauvignon 175 ml:
    This robust red wine boasts strong tannins and rich flavors of blackberry, dark chocolate, and green pepper, often considered a classic pairing with red meats.
  • Pinot Noir 175 ml:
    Light to medium-bodied red wine, celebrated for its delicate structure and bright flavors of cherry, raspberry, and floral notes, often enjoyed slightly chilled.
  • Cognac 50 ml:
    A luxurious type of brandy produced in the Cognac region of France, known for its smoothness and complex flavors, including dried fruit, vanilla, and spices.

Beers

  • Stone Brewing pint - 568 ml:
    Renowned for their bold and hoppy craft beers, Stone Brewing offers a range of styles, including their popular Arrogant Bastard Ale.
  • Guinness pint - 568 ml:
    This iconic Irish stout is famous for its dark color, creamy head, and rich flavor profile, characterized by notes of coffee and chocolate.
  • Lagunitas pint - 568 ml:
    Known for their hop-forward IPAs, Lagunitas Brewing Company offers a variety of innovative brews, with the IPA being a flagship favorite.
  • Peroni 330 ml:
    An Italian pale lager, Peroni is celebrated for its refreshing taste, crisp finish, and hints of citrus, making it a go-to beer for casual occasions.

Liquors

  • Vodka 50 ml:
    A clear spirit distilled from grains or potatoes, vodka is known for its versatility in cocktails, featuring a clean and neutral flavor profile.
  • Rum 50 ml:
    Made from sugarcane or molasses, rum comes in various styles, from light to dark, and is celebrated for its sweet and fruity flavors, often used in tropical cocktails.
  • Whisky 50 ml:
    Aged in wooden casks, whisky presents a wide array of flavors depending on its region and type, known for its rich, smoky, or sweet character.
  • Cognac 50 ml:
    This high-quality brandy from France offers a complex and smooth flavor, making it a favorite for sipping or in upscale cocktails.
