Hot Drinks

Coffees

  • Espresso 30 ml:
    A concentrated coffee brewed by forcing hot water through finely-ground coffee beans, known for its rich flavor and creamy consistency, often served as the base for other coffee drinks.
  • Cappuccino 250 ml:
    A classic coffee drink made with equal parts of espresso, steamed milk, and milk foam, offering a rich and creamy texture that's perfect for coffee lovers.
  • Flat White 250 ml:
    Originating from Australia and New Zealand, this beverage consists of espresso topped with microfoam, creating a velvety coffee experience without the excess milk of a latte.
  • Mocha 250 ml:
    A rich combination of espresso, steamed milk, and chocolate syrup, typically topped with whipped cream, making it a perfect treat for those who love both coffee and chocolate.
  • Americano 250 ml:
    Made by diluting a shot of espresso with hot water, giving it a similar strength to brewed coffee while retaining the distinct espresso flavor.
  • Macchiato 30 ml:
    A shot of espresso "stained" with a small amount of steamed milk or milk foam, focusing on the bold flavor of the coffee.
  • Affogato 150 ml:
    A delightful dessert made by pouring a shot of hot espresso over a scoop of vanilla ice cream or gelato, creating a delicious contrast of hot and cold.

Teas

  • Earl Grey Tea 250 ml:
    A traditional black tea flavored with oil of bergamot, offering a fragrant and unique taste. Often enjoyed with a splash of milk or a twist of lemon.
  • Jasmine Green Tea 250 ml:
    A delicate green tea infused with jasmine blossoms, providing a floral aroma and refreshing flavor, perfect for relaxation.
  • Rooibos Tea 250 ml:
    This caffeine-free herbal tea from South Africa has a naturally sweet and earthy flavor, often enjoyed plain or with milk and honey.
  • Oolong Tea 250 ml:
    A partially oxidized tea that lies between green and black tea, offering a complex flavor profile with floral and fruity notes.
